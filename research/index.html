Critical descaling research and development

Take part in advancing the state of the art in business agility.

Code Without Thrones

This is the cutting edge: two-day Hackathons with zero command & control for groups of up to 160 creating real products by writing real code. For our first experiment, these will be robots that build castles in a multi-user 3D online sandbox. No managers, masters or owners. But metric chapters, consensus treaties, round councils, leadership as a service, descaling metrics ... pure autonomy & alignment to the Dunbar Number and Beyond.

Game without Thrones

We've run this one dozens of times over the past couple of years, but most people haven't experienced it for themselves. Our first proof that self-organizing self-management requires no command & control. Groups of up to 160 collaborate on building a single Westeros-style castle out of lego and paper. With zero managers, masters or owners. But metric chapters, consensus treaties, round councils, leadership as a service, descaling metrics ... if you want to people what descaling business agility can really do, this is the quickest and easiest way.
