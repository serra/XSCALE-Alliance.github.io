Raise your voice

    Alliance members with something XSCALE-related you'd like to post, just add it in the appropriate slack channel and we'll pick it up.

    Or fork the repo, make a post and send a pull-request. PRs don't have to originate in XSCALE - if it's cuttting edge on agile organization we'd love to share it.

    If you're not in the Alliance but just want to have a chat about XSCALE, come spend time in our Telegram group. Sit a spell. Kick your shoes off. Y'all come back, y'hear?

    Jan 27:  XSCALE partnering with BAI
     

    XSCALE Alliance has agreed to a Memorandum of Understanding with Business Agility Institute:

    Business Agility Institute ("BAI") and XSCALE Alliance ("XA") enjoy strong thematic alignment. BAI is an advocacy, membership and research organization that promotes the structure, mindset, and behaviour of an agile organisation. XA is a Linux-style ecosystem of independent service providers that promotes specific agile organisation principles and practice-patterns. This Memorandum is to confirm the commitment of the two to interoperate for the mutual benefit of all members.
