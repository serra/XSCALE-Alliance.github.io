Learn XSCALE Live Online with World Experts on your Couch

Why Do Classrooms Suck?

  • You have to take time off work even when work won't wait.
  • You cram learning into a few days with no time for homework or research.
  • Your trainers don't have real experience so can't answer real questions.
  • If you're late to a session, the trainers can't take time to catch you up.

These problems don't occur live online.

  • We run these sessions outside work hours, so you can keep working.
  • We run 3-hour sessions twice a week so you have time for homework and research.
  • We're not trainers - we're working professionals with decades in the field.
  • If you miss a session, you can catch it a month later just like a train.

Why Not Train Everyone?

Change programs that try to train everyone at once magnify risks of disruption and compromise. Clients experience cognitive dissonance while the consultancies they pay declare their change is now "working". Per the emperor's new clothes, no employee dares say it is not.

XSCALE recommends an much simpler change srtategy. Self-propagating transformation starts small and fast, not big and slow, delivers learning by immersion instead of instruction, proves new capabilities as it grows them, and prioritizes change bottlenecks as they occur. It makes business agility your first priority rather than an afterthought.

While it's counterproductive to train everyone at once, we use training to bring the pioneers of a transformation up to speed. Then they have use for training collateral too as they become change leaders. This is why all the XSCALE training decks are distributed to students under Creative Commons licenses.

Coaches Included

You can't train someone to be an agile coach. People develop coaching skills by practicing them in the field. It takes years of real world experience, not training, to become qualified as a Coach in the XSCALE learning ecosystem.

As a practical skill, there are no shortcuts, but our ecosystem of independent coaches share learnings continuously and collaborate on making them available as open training collateral. XSCALE Stewards mentor and consult with XSCALE's community of coaches to bring decades of perspective to the generation of working business capability, not a greasy pole of certificates.
