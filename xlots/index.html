Learn XSCALE Live Online with World Experts from your Couch

Why Do Classrooms Suck?

  • You have to take time off work even when work won't wait.
  • You cram learning into a day or two with no time for reflection or homework.
  • Your trainers don't have real experience so can't answer real questions.
  • If you're late to a session, the trainers can't take time to catch you up.

These problems don't occur live online.

  • We run these sessions outside work hours, so you can keep working.
  • We run 3-hour sessions twice a week so you have time for homework and research.
  • We're not trainers - we're professional coaches with decades in the field.
  • If you miss a session, you can catch it a month later just like a train.

Don't Train Everyone

Change programs that try to train everyone at once magnify risks of disruption and compromise. Clients experience cognitive dissonance as these things become the norm while their consultants declare the change is "working". Per the emperor's new clothes, no employee dares say it is not.

XSCALE provides an simpler change strategy. Self-propagating transformation starts small and fast, not big and slow, delivers learning by immersion instead of instruction, proves new capabilities as it grows them, and prioritizes change bottlenecks as they occur. It makes business agility your first priority rather than an afterthought.

While it's counterproductive to train everyone at once, training is essential to bring the pioneers of a transformation up to speed. And they have use for training collateral in turn as they become change leaders. This is why the XSCALE training decks are distributed to students under Creative Commons licenses.

Coaches Included

You can't train someone to be an agile coach. People develop coaching skills by practicing them in the field. It takes years of real world experience, not training, to become qualified as a Coach in the XSCALE learning ecosystem.

As a practical skill, there are no shortcuts, but our ecosystem of independent coaches share learnings continuously and collaborate on making them available as open training collateral. XSCALE Stewards mentor and consult with XSCALE's community of coaches to bring decades of perspective to the generation of working business capability, not a greasy pole of certificates.

Live Online Training Stream Attendance Policies

  1. Online training streams are sequential: each session is a pre-requisite for those that come after. A session will be of no benefit to you if you have not attended the pre-requisite sessions.
  2. Seats in online training streams are strictly limited to ten (10) places due to the inherent limitations of online conferencing software. In person, people can easily interrupt each other. In larger online classes this is impossible, which would unacceptably limit the benefit of this live training.
  3. Due to these inherent constraints, if you miss one of the the scheduled sessions in your training stream you may *NOT* attend subsequent sessions in the same stream.
  4. In the unfortunate event that you miss a session, your Coach, at their sole discretion and at a price determined by them, may arrange a special make-up session for you in order for you to rejoin the stream.
  5. Otherwise, you'll need to book a place on a subsequent stream if you wish to continue your training to certification. In summary this works like missing a plane - you can't just get on another one without paying for another seat because that wouldn't respect the needs of the other paying passengers.
  6. In the unlikely event that your Coach is unable to deliver a training session, however, a replacement session will be made available to you by your Coach at no extra charge. In the still more unlikely event that your Coach is incapacitated, you will be provided with your choice of replacement training streams at no extra charge.
  7. Certification in this course is based on homework, not exams. Certificates cannot be issued to participants who do not attend all sessions of their training stream, or who do not complete the homework.
  8. Please inquire here if you would like to move your booking from one training stream at least one week prior to commencing your training. We will always seek to accommodate your needs, but note that extra places beyond those advertised cannot be guaranteed and refunds are not available under any circumstances.
